About Us


Who We Are

We are a restaurant business based in our home kitchen in the London Borough of Brent. A Taste of the Original Jamaican cuisine that will make you feel back at home. Established in 2023, Founded by a chef named Tildon Davis who has had decades of experience in cooking and serving customers the authentic taste through very famous events such as Notting Hill Carnival and African Pop Up Markets.


Board House Kitchen has been planned for decades to serve the whole of London with flavorsome dishes o bring the Taste of the Caribbean more into the UK spotlight. We aim to attract customers from all over the world to make the Jamaican culture recognized and appreciated.


At Board-House Kitchen, we believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a shared philosophy that fosters community and passion. Our unique culinary experiences immerse you in the art of cooking, allowing you to bond with fellow food lovers while learning from passionate chefs.
