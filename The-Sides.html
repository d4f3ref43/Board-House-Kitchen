Festival

What is Festival?

Festival is a sweet tasting side dish coming from Jamaica, The Caribbean. This is similar to fried dumplings but less round and more sweet. This dish can be added with Jerk Chicken, Escovitch Fish, Oxtail and more.

Festival contains:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal
  • Vanilla

Rice and Peas

What is Rice and Peas?

Rice and Peas is a dish originated from Jamaica, The Caribbean containing Rice and Kidney Beans (or even Gungo Peas). This is one of the well known dish to have with Jerk Chicken, Curry Goat, Escovitch Fish, Fried Chicken, Chickpeas ,Oxtail and more.

Rice and Peas is considered one of the most common dishes, and it is most likely the first that comes to mind of customers.

NOTE: Rice and Peas can differentiate in the type of peas used which may also change it in color it can either be Gungo Peas or Kidney Beans. Kidney Beans is favoured more by customers than Gungo itself.

Rice and Peas consists of:

  • Basmati Rice
  • Kidney Beans (or Gungo Peas)
  • Coconut Milk
  • Escallion and Onions
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper

This Dish is suitable to vegans
