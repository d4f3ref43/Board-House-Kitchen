What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and store certain information to improve your browsing experience.

Types of cookies used:

Analytical, Functional, Advertising.

Purpose of each cookie:

Analytical Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how visitors interact with our website, so we can improve its performance and user experience. For example, we may track the number of visitors to our website or how long users stay on certain pages.

Functional Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences, such as language settings, and provide a personalized experience.

Advertising Cookies: We may use these cookies to serve targeted ads based on your interests or behavior on our website.

First and Third-party cookies:

First-party cookies: These are cookies that are set by our website (e.g. a reminder of your shopping cart or preferences).

Third-party cookies: These are set by external services, such as Google Analytics or Instagram, or other third-party platforms that we may use for marketing, tracking, and improving our website’s performance.

Data collection and processing:

Data is being processed through checkouts and sign-ins and stored securely on a cloud database system. We use Google Analytics to track website activity, including the number of visitors, how they use our website, and other metrics that help us improve our website’s performance.

Duration of cookies:

Each cookie will remain active as long as 2 hours maximum to give user extensive time to read, navigate and deciding on an order they want to make.

User consent:

Users have two options when it comes to interacting with cookies. They can 'Accept' it or they can 'Reject' it.

Opt-out options:

Consent: By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy.

Disabling Cookies: You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings. However, please note that some features of our website may not function correctly if you disable cookies. The process to manage cookies varies by browser, but you can usually find instructions in the settings menu of your browser.

Users can also manage their preferences with target-advertising whether if they could opt-out of it or not.

Compliance with regulations:

GDPR and ePrivacy. With these regulations, we collect and store your data only with your consent, and we protect your privacy by not sharing any personally identifiable information without your permission.
